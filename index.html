How to keep your houseplants alive

Watering

Overwatering causes more damage than underwatering. Check soil moisture with your finger or a moisture meter before watering.

Light

Match plants to light conditions. Most prefer bright, indirect light. South-facing windows can be too harsh, while north-facing may be too dark.

Soil & Potting

Always use pots with drainage holes to prevent root rot. Use a premium, well-draining potting mix. Repot every 12–18 months if the plant becomes root-bound.
